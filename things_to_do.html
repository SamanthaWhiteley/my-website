The different aspects of the resort:

At Crescent Bar Resort, adventure awaits around every corner, promising endless opportunities for fun and relaxation. Dive into the refreshing waters of the Columbia River and indulge in an array of thrilling water sports, from jet skiing and paddleboarding to kayaking and fishing. For those seeking land-based adventures, explore scenic hiking trails that wind through lush landscapes, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. Unwind poolside, soak up the sun's rays, or hit the beach. Golf enthusiasts can perfect their swing on the pristine greens of the nearby golf courses, while tennis courts offer a chance to engage in friendly matches under the clear blue sky. Families will delight in the variety of activities tailored to all ages, from mini-golf and beach volleyball to the annual Fourth of July parade. Embark on a leisurely bike ride along winding paths, or gather around a crackling bonfire for s'mores and stargazing under the twinkling night sky. For those with a taste for adventure, nearby attractions such as the towering cliffs of the Wanapum State Park and the thrilling whitewater rapids of the Wenatchee River beckon exploration. And as the day winds down, gather with loved ones for a delicious meal at one of Crescent Bar's restaurants. No matter your passion or preference, Crescent Bar Resort offers an unparalleled blend of excitement and relaxation, ensuring memories that will last a lifetime.

The Beaches

Nestled along the serene shores of the Columbia River, Crescent Bar boasts a collection of breathtaking beaches, each offering its own unique charm and allure. Whether you seek a secluded spot for quiet reflection or a vibrant hub of activity, there's a beach to suit every preference. Sink your toes into the soft sands of Crescent Bar Beach, where families can bask in the sunshine while children build sandcastles and splash in the gentle waters. For those seeking adventure, Rooster's Landing Beach provides access to thrilling water sports such as jet skiing, paddleboarding, and kayaking, all against the backdrop of stunning riverside scenery. Meanwhile, Sunset Beach offers a tranquil escape, perfect for couples seeking a romantic retreat or individuals looking to unwind with a good book and a breathtaking view of the sun dipping below the horizon. With a variety of beaches to explore, Crescent Bar invites visitors to discover the beauty and tranquility of its pristine waterfronts, promising endless opportunities for relaxation and recreation alike.

What was nearby

The Ice Cream Parlor

There was an ice cream parlor that was just outside of the gates to the resort called Island Cup and Cone. When visiting Crescent Bar my family would come to the ice cream shop at least once during each trip. My sister and I loved the flavor cotton candy, and we would get the same thing every time.

The Golf Course

There was a golf course in Crescent Bar and they had a golf shop next to the ice cream shop as well. My Grandpa loved to golf and would spend all day on the course when he was staying at Crescent Bar. Not many other people in my family visited the course very often.

The Convenience Store

There was a little store that had mostly snacks, but you could get some essentials here as well. If you wanted to go to an actual grocery store you would have to drive up to Quincy, a nearby town to the grocery stores there.

The Pizza Parlor

There was a pizza shop called Tower Pizza. My family and I would go to this pizza shop at least once per trip to Crescent Bar.

The Swimsuit Shop

There was a small shop that sold bathing suits on the corner. My sister and I would always want to look at what they were selling, and our second cousin Kelsey worked there over the summer for a couple of years.
